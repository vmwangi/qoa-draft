Archdiocesan Pastoral Council Meeting

Date: April 20, 2023

Location: Cathedral

The Archdiocesan Pastoral Council will convene for its quarterly meeting. Key agenda items include pastoral planning updates, financial reports, and discussions on upcoming archdiocesan initiatives. All parish pastoral council chairpersons and secretaries are required to attend. Please arrive by 9:00 AM for registration. Lunch will be provided.

For more information, please contact the Archdiocesan office.

×

Archdiocesan Youth Conference

Dates: June 15-18, 2023

Location: [Specify Location]

The annual Archdiocesan Youth Conference is a vibrant gathering for young Catholics across the Archdiocese. The conference will feature inspiring speakers, workshops, prayer sessions, music, and fellowship. It's a great opportunity for youth to deepen their faith and connect with peers.

Registration is open to all youth ages 13-18. Please contact your parish youth coordinator for registration forms and details on transportation.

Early bird registration deadline: May 15, 2023.

Archdiocesan Pilgrimage to Holy Land

Dates: October 10-20, 2023

Embark on a spiritual journey to the Holy Land with fellow pilgrims from the Archdiocese. Visit sacred sites, walk in the footsteps of Jesus, and deepen your connection to the roots of our faith. The pilgrimage package includes flights, accommodation, meals, guided tours, and spiritual reflections.

Limited spots available. Interested parishioners should contact their parish office or the Archdiocesan Pilgrimage Office for detailed itineraries and registration information.

Deadline for registration: August 30, 2023.
