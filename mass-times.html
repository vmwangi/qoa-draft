Special Masses & Celebrations

In addition to our regular Mass schedule, we celebrate special Masses throughout the liturgical year.

Holy Days of Obligation

Vigil: 5:30 PM | Day of: 6:30 AM, 12:00 PM, 5:30 PM
Center Church

Holy Days of Obligation are special feast days on which Catholics are obligated to attend Mass. These include the Solemnity of Mary (January 1), the Assumption (August 15), All Saints Day (November 1), the Immaculate Conception (December 8), and Christmas (December 25).

Ash Wednesday

6:30 AM, 12:00 PM, 5:30 PM
Center Church

Ash Wednesday marks the beginning of Lent, a 40-day period of prayer, fasting, and almsgiving in preparation for Easter. Ashes are distributed at all Masses as a sign of our mortality and need for repentance.

Holy Week & Easter

Palm Sunday, Holy Thursday, Good Friday, Easter Vigil, Easter Sunday
Center Church (with some services at outstations)

Holy Week commemorates the final days of Jesus' life, his passion, death, and resurrection. Special liturgies are celebrated throughout the week, culminating in the Easter Vigil on Saturday night and Easter Sunday Masses.

Parish Feast Day

Feast of Mary, Queen of Apostles (Saturday after Ascension)
10:00 AM Solemn Mass
Center Church

We celebrate our parish feast day with a special Mass followed by community festivities. All parishioners from all outstations gather together for this celebration.
