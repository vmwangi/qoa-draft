About St. Gabriel Church

Goal: To nurture a faith-filled community in the Githurai area through worship, catechesis, and outreach.

St. Gabriel Church, an outstation of Queen of Apostles Parish, was established in 1990 to serve the Catholic faithful in Githurai. Known for its welcoming atmosphere, it offers a range of spiritual and community activities to strengthen the faith of its members.
