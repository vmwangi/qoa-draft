About MYM

The Missionary Youth Movement (MYM) is a Catholic youth organization that focuses on missionary formation and evangelization. It aims to help young people discover their baptismal call to be missionaries in their daily lives.

MYM was founded in 1984 and has since spread to many countries around the world. Our parish chapter was established in 2005 and has been active in various missionary activities both locally and internationally.

The four pillars of MYM are:

  • Prayer: Developing a deep prayer life and relationship with God
  • Formation: Learning about the Catholic faith and missionary spirituality
  • Community: Building a supportive community of young missionaries
  • Mission: Engaging in missionary activities and evangelization

MYM is open to young people ages 14-25 who want to deepen their faith and share it with others. We meet every Sunday at 3:00 PM in the Parish Hall.

×

MYM Activities

MYM engages in a variety of activities throughout the year to help members grow in faith and develop as missionaries. Here are some of our regular activities:

  • Weekly Meetings: Every Sunday at 3:00 PM in the Parish Hall. These meetings include prayer, formation, sharing, and planning for upcoming activities.
  • Bible Study: Every Wednesday at 6:00 PM in the Conference Room. We study Scripture together to deepen our understanding of God's Word.
  • Outreach Programs: Monthly visits to local orphanages, hospitals, and elderly homes to share God's love through service.
  • Mission Trips: Annual mission trips to different parts of the country to engage in evangelization and service projects.
  • Retreats: Quarterly retreats for spiritual renewal and deepening of faith.
  • Social Events: Regular social events to build community and have fun together.

All young people ages 14-25 are welcome to participate in these activities. For more information, contact Sarah Nduta at +254 123 456 789.

About PMC

The Pontifical Mission Childhood (PMC) is a Catholic children's organization that helps children learn about the global Church and develop a missionary spirit from an early age. It encourages children to pray and share with children in mission countries.

PMC was founded in 1843 by Bishop Charles de Forbin-Janson in France and has since spread to many countries around the world. Our parish chapter was established in 2010 and has been active in various missionary activities.

The four pillars of PMC are:

  • Prayer: Encouraging children to pray for children in mission countries
  • Learning: Learning about the global Church and different cultures
  • Sharing: Sharing resources with children in mission countries
  • Service: Serving others in the spirit of Christ

PMC is open to children ages 5-13. We meet every Saturday at 10:00 AM in the Children's Room.

×

PMC Activities

PMC engages in a variety of activities throughout the year to help children learn about the global Church and develop a missionary spirit. Here are some of our regular activities:

  • Weekly Meetings: Every Saturday at 10:00 AM in the Children's Room. These meetings include prayer, learning about mission countries, arts and crafts, and games.
  • Mission Rosary: Praying the World Mission Rosary for children around the world.
  • Cultural Days: Learning about different cultures and countries through food, music, dance, and crafts.
  • Fundraising: Collecting coins and organizing small fundraisers to support children in mission countries.
  • Liturgical Participation: Participating in special Masses and liturgical celebrations throughout the year.
  • Mission Animation: Presenting mission awareness programs to the parish community.

All children ages 5-13 are welcome to participate in these activities. Parents are also encouraged to get involved as volunteers. For more information, contact Mary Wanjiku at +254 123 456 790.

About Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry provides a space for young people to grow in faith, develop leadership skills, and build lasting friendships. We offer a variety of programs and activities that address the spiritual, social, and emotional needs of teenagers.

Our parish Youth Ministry was established in 2000 and has been a vibrant part of our parish community ever since. We focus on creating a welcoming environment where young people can explore their faith, ask questions, and develop a personal relationship with Jesus Christ.

The four pillars of our Youth Ministry are:

  • Catechesis: Learning about the Catholic faith and growing in knowledge of God
  • Prayer and Worship: Developing a personal prayer life and participating in liturgical celebrations
  • Community Life: Building friendships and a sense of belonging in the Church
  • Service: Putting faith into action through service to others

Our Youth Ministry is open to all teenagers ages 13-18. We meet every Friday at 5:30 PM in the Youth Room.

×

Youth Activities

Our Youth Ministry offers a variety of activities throughout the year to help young people grow in faith and develop leadership skills. Here are some of our regular activities:

  • Weekly Meetings: Every Friday at 5:30 PM in the Youth Room. These meetings include prayer, faith formation, discussions, games, and social time.
  • Retreats: Quarterly retreats for spiritual renewal and deepening of faith.
  • Service Projects: Monthly service projects to put faith into action and serve those in need.
  • Social Events: Regular social events like movie nights, sports tournaments, and game nights to build community and have fun together.
  • Diocesan Events: Participation in diocesan youth events like rallies, conferences, and camps.
  • Leadership Development: Opportunities for young people to develop leadership skills and take on leadership roles within the ministry.

All teenagers ages 13-18 are welcome to participate in these activities. For more information, contact John Kamau at +254 123 456 792.
