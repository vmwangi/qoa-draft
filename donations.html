Tithe - Center Church

Tithing is the practice of donating 10% of your income to support the work of the Church. It is a biblical practice that acknowledges God as the source of all blessings.

In Malachi 3:10, God challenges us: "Bring the whole tithe into the storehouse, that there may be food in my house. Test me in this," says the LORD Almighty, "and see if I will not throw open the floodgates of heaven and pour out so much blessing that there will not be room enough to store it."

Your tithe supports:

  • Parish operations and maintenance
  • Staff salaries
  • Ministry programs
  • Outreach and charitable works

Mass Intentions - Center Church

Mass intentions are donations given when requesting a Mass to be celebrated for a specific intention, such as for the repose of a soul, for a special intention, or in thanksgiving.

To request a Mass intention, please visit the parish office or contact us by phone or email. The suggested donation for a Mass intention is Specify Amount.

When requesting a Mass intention, please provide:

  • Your name and contact information
  • The name(s) of the person(s) for whom the Mass is being offered
  • The specific intention (e.g., for the repose of the soul of..., for the health of..., in thanksgiving for...)
  • Your preferred date and time (we will do our best to accommodate your request)

Mass intentions are a beautiful way to pray for your loved ones and support the ministry of the Church.
