Our Parish Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To build a community of faith that celebrates God's love, nurtures spiritual growth, and reaches out to those in need through service and evangelization.

Our Vision

To be a welcoming parish where all people can encounter Christ, grow in their relationship with God, and be empowered to serve others.

Our Values

We are guided by faith, hope, and love. We value community, service, inclusivity, and a deep commitment to the teachings of Christ.

Welcome from our Parish Priest

Dear brothers and sisters in Christ,

I warmly welcome you to Queen of Apostles Parish. Our parish is a vibrant community of faith where we strive to live the Gospel values and share God's love with one another. Whether you are a long-time parishioner or a visitor, we hope you find a spiritual home with us.

At Queen of Apostles, we are committed to nurturing the spiritual growth of all our members through meaningful liturgies, faith formation programs, and opportunities for service. We believe that each person is called to use their unique gifts to build up the Body of Christ and to make a positive difference in our world.

I invite you to explore our website to learn more about our parish, our ministries, and our upcoming events. If you have any questions or if there is any way we can be of assistance to you, please do not hesitate to contact our parish office.

May God bless you abundantly, and may Our Lady, Queen of Apostles, intercede for you and your loved ones.

Fr. Charles Okeyo
