CMA & CWA

Strengthening Faith and Community

Parish CMA

The Catholic Men's Association (CMA) at Queen of Apostles Parish is a vibrant community of men dedicated to spiritual growth, fellowship, and service. Through regular meetings, retreats, and charitable activities, CMA members support each other in living out their faith and contributing to the parish and wider community.

What We Do

  • Monthly spiritual retreats and formation
  • Community service and outreach programs
  • Support for parish initiatives and events
  • Mentorship for young men in the parish

Parish CWA

The Catholic Women's Association (CWA) fosters a sisterhood of faith, prayer, and action among women in our parish. The CWA organizes spiritual events, community outreach, and support for families, empowering women to live their faith vibrantly and serve those in need.

What We Do

  • Regular prayer meetings and spiritual formation
  • Family support and counseling services
  • Charitable works and community outreach
  • Parish event organization and support
  • Mentorship for young women in the parish
